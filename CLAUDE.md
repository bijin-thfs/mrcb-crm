# MRCB Staff CRM

Internal CRM for Mayyanad Regional Co-operative Bank staff (clerks, managers, admins). Desktop-first web app that processes account applications from the customer PWA.

## Supabase Backend (shared with mayyanad-bank PWA)
- Project: `ipydxexifkxyldcgjroo.supabase.co`
- Client: `src/lib/supabase.ts`
- Schema: `supabase/schema.sql`

## Design System
Same "Modern Heritage" design system as the customer PWA:
- Primary: #001833 (Deep Navy), Secondary: #775A19 (Heritage Gold)
- Fonts: Manrope (headlines), Inter (body)
- Icons: Material Symbols Outlined
- Bilingual: English primary + Malayalam subtitles where appropriate
